Research

I have conducted extensive research in the field of electronic devices and circuits, focusing on the fabrication and characterization of nanostructured electronic devices. My work includes the study of semiconductor materials for high-efficiency solar cells and the development of thin-film transistors. During my academic journey, I have contributed to multiple publications in reputed journals and presented my findings at international conferences. My research involves material synthesis, structural characterization, and performance evaluation using advanced spectroscopy techniques. I am passionate about exploring innovative technologies to enhance the efficiency and reliability of electronic systems.
